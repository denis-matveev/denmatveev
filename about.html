About

Practical design with a strong eye for structure.

I’m Denis Matveev, a product and UX/UI designer who likes turning loose ideas into clear interfaces and portfolio stories that feel intentional.

My focus is on product clarity, component systems, and presentation. I like work that looks good, but more importantly, work that helps people understand the product quickly.

This static portfolio is intentionally lightweight: no framework, no build step, and no extra dependencies. That keeps the site easy to host, easy to edit, and easy to extend.

Capabilities

  • Product design and UX/UI
  • Design systems and reusable components
  • Case study writing and portfolio polish
  • Responsive static site setup for GitHub Pages
Based on a minimal, editable static structure. Designed to stay easy to maintain.
